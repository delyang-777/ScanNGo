Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how our system collects, uses, and protects personal information provided by users. We value your privacy and are committed to maintaining the confidentiality and security of your data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect basic user information such as your name, email address, and account credentials. For attendance and event tracking, we may also record scan logs, timestamps, and participation details.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to manage system access, verify attendance, monitor activity logs, and improve overall functionality. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with unauthorized parties.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to safeguard your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. User passwords are securely hashed and stored.

4. Data Access and Control

Users can request updates or deletion of their data by contacting the system administrator. Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

5. Policy Updates

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be communicated within the system or through user notifications.

6. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact the administrator or system support team.
